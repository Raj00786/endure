Powered by a gasoline engine the platform brings together the efficiency and agility of helicopter along with the energy density of gasoline. </p>
                      <p>Robust auto-pilot controller. Making fully autonomous flight, Loiter / Autonomous Hover at specified altitude, circle radius and location, Waypoint navigation, return to home on loss of radio contact. Auto takeoff and landing, rugged laptop based ground station for mission planning and telemetry, fully ready and tested.</p>

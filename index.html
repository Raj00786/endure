HOW IT WORKS

At Endure Air we are working on solving the endurance problem of drones and making them fully autonomous…

90 Mins Flight endurance

>3kgs payload capability

Vertical takeoff and landing

GPS enabled auto landing and takeoff

Onboard autopilot and AI computers

Step 1: Order received along with GPS Coordinates, along with satellite image of destination

Step 2: Load payload and Take off

Step 3: Find exact destination, once at the GPS coordinates matches the satellite image to the destination

Step 4: Deliver finds a safe drop location away from cars, roofs, trees and people.
